Many of these blog entries are written in a hurry and are thus very informal. While I try to offer good explanations, not all posts are suited for learning a topic from scratch. Many serve as summaries of relevant mathematics. With prior knowledge, they provide helpful reviews. My main objective is to summarize material, so i can review it when needed.

Additionally, do not believe that this is the result of independent, original or novel research. Standing on the shoulders of giants, i extract information from papers, videos, and online content. Some posts mention the sources, but I may have missed many others. If you’re interested in a topic, I’m happy to point you toward researchers and resources.
