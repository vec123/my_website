Many of these blog entries are written in a hurry and are thus very informal. While I try to offer good explanations, not all posts are suited for learning a topic from scratch. Many serve as summaries of relevant mathematics. With prior knowledge, they provide helpful reviews.

Additionally, do not believe that this is the result of independent research. I stand on the shoulders of giants, drawing from papers, videos, and online content. Some posts mention sources, but I may have missed others. If you’re interested in a topic, I’m happy to point you toward researchers and resources.
