Quantum Information Equations

1. State Representation:

\[ |\Psi\rangle = \sum_{k=0}^{d-1} \psi_k |k\rangle \]

\[ |\Phi\rangle = \sum_{k=0}^{d-1} \phi_k |k\rangle \]

\[ P_\Phi = |\langle \Phi | \Psi \rangle|^2 \]

2. Operators:

\[ \Pi = |\Psi\rangle \langle \Psi| \quad \text{where } \Pi^2 = \Pi \]

\[ \hat{O} = |\Phi\rangle \langle \Psi| \]

3. Measurement & Observables:

\[ \hat{O} = \sum_{k=0}^{d-1} \lambda_k |k\rangle \langle k| \]

\[ \langle \hat{O} \rangle = \sum_{k=0}^{d-1} \lambda_k P_{k,\Phi} \]

4. Observables for Qubits:

\[ \sigma_x = \begin{pmatrix} 0 & 1 \\ 1 & 0 \end{pmatrix}, \quad \sigma_y = \begin{pmatrix} 0 & -i \\ i & 0 \end{pmatrix}, \quad \sigma_z = \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & -1 \end{pmatrix} \]

5. Mixed States:

\[ \rho = \sum_k p_k |\psi_k\rangle \langle \psi_k| \]

\[ \text{Tr}(\rho^2) < 1 \quad \text{(Mixed)}, \quad \text{Tr}(\rho^2) = 1 \quad \text{(Pure)} \]

6. Entanglement Criterion:

\[ \rho_{AB} \neq \sum_{x} p_x \rho_{A,x} \otimes \rho_{B,x} \quad \text{(Entangled)} \]

7. Quantum Fourier Transform:

\[ \text{QFT}|x\rangle = \frac{1}{\sqrt{N}} \sum_{k=0}^{N-1} e^{2\pi i \frac{xk}{N}} |k\rangle \]
